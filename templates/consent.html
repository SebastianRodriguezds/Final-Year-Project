Privacy Policy and Consent

Welcome to our platform. We are committed to supporting your mental well-being. To be transparent, we want to inform you about the data we collect and how it will be used to assist you in managing your work-life balance and overall mental health.

1. Information We Collect

  • Work Hours: We track your work start and end times to help you maintain a healthy work schedule and prevent burnout.
  • Activity Logs: We monitor tool and application usage to identify stress points or signs of overwork and offer timely interventions.
  • Productivity Scores: These are measured to understand your workload and ensure you're not overwhelmed, while helping you improve your efficiency without compromising your well-being.
  • Keystroke Monitoring: This is solely for statistical purposes and helps track periods of intense activity or prolonged stress, so we can suggest breaks or mental health resources.
  • Screen Time: We analyze the time you spend on screen to encourage healthier habits and ensure you're taking regular breaks to prevent digital fatigue.
  • Email Analysis: We track email volume and response times to identify when you might be overburdened, providing support or reminders to prioritize your mental health.
